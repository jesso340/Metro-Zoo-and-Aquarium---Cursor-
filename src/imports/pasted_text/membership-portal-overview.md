1. 🧭 Overview
The Membership Portal enables members to stay engaged and derive value from their membership—even when they cannot attend in-person events—through interactive livestreams, agent-guided experiences, and reward-based engagement loops.
This feature transforms passive viewing into an interactive, personalized, and rewarding experience, driving higher engagement, emotional connection, and renewal likelihood.

2. 🎯 Goals
Increase member engagement outside of physical visits
Drive emotional connection to membership
Improve retention and early renewals
Reinforce perceived membership value through visible rewards and impact

3. 👤 Target User
Primary Persona: Grace
Parent with young kids
Values meaningful, educational experiences
Wants flexibility and convenience
Motivated by value, family engagement, and impact

4. 💡 Core Value Proposition
“I can’t attend” → becomes → “I’m still part of this”
Passive content → becomes → interactive, rewarding participation
Membership → becomes → ongoing value + emotional connection
